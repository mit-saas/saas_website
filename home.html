South Asian Association of Students

Welcome to MIT SAAS!

We are the MIT South Asian Association of students, one of the largest student groups on campus representing Indians, Pakistanis, Bangladeshis, Sri Lankans, Nepalis, and other South Asians at MIT. SAAS strives to foster a sense of community at MIT for all students interested in getting a taste of South Asian culture! Find out more about us, board, and our annual events above, and check out our most recent upcoming event below! Feel free to reach out to us at saasboard@mit.edu with any questions you might have!

C-SHOW 2018

C-Show 2018 is here! MIT SAAS's Spring Cultural Show is a time-honored tradition, and it is back for 2018! This year, C-Show will take place on Friday, April 6th from 8pm to 10pm (doors open at 7:30). We will be selling tickets in Lobby 10 the week of April 6th as well as on AdmitOne. Check back soon for more information! The show will feature dance performances from student groups, music, and the traditional class dances choreographed and performed by your very own class! Sign up here to participate in another year of C-Show!! Everyone is welcome! We hope to see you there :)
